The Traveling Lens

My travels have taken me across the world and back. From South America to the remote reaches of Iceland, my aim has always been to capture and experience the world. So with flight of foot, a camera in my pack, and a steady appetite for new sights, I continue to set off around the planet in search of inspiration. These are my travels thus far.

Iceland
My main method for seeing the world has always been to hike or backpack to those landscapes or features that inspire beauty or awe. Over an extended trip to Iceland, I did a series of hikes that led me across the interior of the island, and around the ring road that circles the exterior of the country.
Costa Rica
A brief two week work stint in Costa Rica allowed me the opportunity to see the bustling city of San Jose, the dense rain forests of Central America, and the tucked-away beaches of the Pacific.
