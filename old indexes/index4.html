About

Welcome to Think North, the visual portfolio my (Tim Cheneval) wide breadth of work. Think North embodies my fascination with historical story telling, with a passion for communicating a rich heritage of pure values. Think North is inspired by the stories in the constellations, where early man originally wrote their sagas to give personality and memory to their navigational tools in the sky.

My fascination with stories and how they are told doesn't stop with the constellations. In my personal time, I do everything I can to get out into the wild, in order to build the experiences and memories necessary to write my own stories.
